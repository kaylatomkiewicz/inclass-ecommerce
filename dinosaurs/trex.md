The *Tyrannosaurus* Rex is the most **fierce** dinosaur that ever existed.

The T-Rex lived in the [Late Cretaceous Period](http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/142729/Cretaceous-Period).

## Features

- Big Teeth
- Small Arms
- Thick Tail
